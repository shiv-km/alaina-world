# 🌟 Alaina's World

A collection of fun mini-games for kids, built as a single self-contained HTML file — no installs, no dependencies. Just open in a browser and play!

---

## 🎮 Games

### 🖥️ Computer Games *(keyboard required)*

| Game | How to Play |
|---|---|
| 🎹 Desktop Piano | Press any letter key (A–Z) to play a note. Keys light up and letters float upward. |
| 🎈 Letter Bounce | Press any key to drop that letter. Letters bounce around the screen. |

### 🎨 Drawing & Creative

| Game | How to Play |
|---|---|
| 🎨 Magic Painter | Drag to draw rainbow brush strokes on a white canvas. Press 🧹 Clear to start over. |
| ✨ Paint Glow | Drag to draw glowing neon lines with sound. Press 🧹 Clear to start over. |
| 🎆 Fireworks | Tap anywhere to launch a firework rocket that explodes into colorful particles. |
| 🪄 Magic Wand | Drag to spray sparkles and butterflies that drift and fade away. |

### 🏃 Catching & Reflex

| Game | How to Play |
|---|---|
| 🍎 Catch Fruit | Move your finger/mouse to slide the basket and catch falling fruit. |
| ❄️ Snowflake Catch | Slide the snowman to catch falling snowflakes — each one plays a chime note. |
| 🦋 Butterflies | Tap fluttering butterflies to catch them before they flutter away. |
| ☁️ Cloud Jumper | Tap falling raindrops before they hit the ground. Don't let them splash! |
| ⭐ Star Catcher | Tap or swipe through glowing stars before they fade away. |
| 🫧 Bubble Rescue | Tap the bubbles to pop them and free the creatures inside. |
| 🐸 Frog Jump | Tap anywhere to shoot the frog's tongue and catch the flies. |
| 💥 Pop Everything | You have 30 seconds — tap to pop as many bouncing emojis as possible! |

### 🎵 Music & Sounds

| Game | How to Play |
|---|---|
| 📱 Phone Piano | Tap or slide across the colored bars to play musical notes (Do Re Mi…). |
| 🥁 Drum Kit | Tap the zones to play kick, snare, hi-hat, cymbal, and tom drum sounds. |
| 🎵 Song Builder | Tap grid squares to toggle notes on/off and build a looping melody. Press 🧹 Clear to reset. |
| 🌈 Rainbow Slide | Move your finger up and down to change the color and play a sliding tone. |

### 📚 Learn & Play

| Game | How to Play |
|---|---|
| 🔤 Talking ABC | Each letter appears with its word and emoji. Tap to advance, or wait for auto-cycle. |
| 🔢 Number Bounce | Tap anywhere to drop a random number that bounces and speaks aloud. |
| 🎨 Color Match | A color name is shown — tap the matching colored box as fast as you can. Gets harder! |

---

## 🚀 Running

Just open `index.html` in any modern browser. Works on desktop, tablet, and phone.
