🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

✅ Short version: We don't collect any personal information. No accounts, no emails, no tracking. Everything stays on your device.

1. Who We Are

Alaina's World is a free browser-based entertainment and learning platform for young children, available at shiv-km.github.io/alaina-world. It is an independent personal project, not a commercial entity.

2. What Information We Collect

We collect no personal information. We do not ask for names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other identifying information.

The only data stored is kept entirely within your own browser's localStorage — it never leaves your device and is never transmitted to us:

  • ⭐ Stars count (reward points earned through gameplay)
  • 🔥 Daily streak count and last-played date
  • 👤 Display name and avatar emoji (if you choose to set these)
  • 🎮 Number of games played (local counter)
  • 📖 Custom stories you generate (stored locally as text)
  • 🔑 OpenAI API key (only if you voluntarily add one for AI features — see Section 4)
  • 🔓 Unlocked premium coloring themes
📱 You can clear all stored data at any time by going to your browser's Settings → Clear Site Data, or by using the browser's developer tools.

3. Cookies & Tracking

We use no cookies, no analytics scripts, no tracking pixels, and no advertising networks. We do not track your behavior, browsing history, or usage patterns.

4. Optional AI Features (OpenAI)

Some features — AI Story Creator and AI Coloring Studio — optionally use the OpenAI API. These features are entirely opt-in and require you to provide your own OpenAI API key.

  • Your API key is stored only in your browser's localStorage
  • It is sent directly from your browser to api.openai.com when you use an AI feature — not through our servers (we have no servers)
  • OpenAI's own privacy policy governs how they handle the content of those requests
  • You can remove your API key at any time from the Settings button in Story Time or the Coloring Studio
  • You can use all 28 games, all 15 stories, and 4 coloring themes without ever providing an API key

OpenAI's privacy policy is available at openai.com/privacy.

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Alaina's World is designed for children and is fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • We do not collect any personal information from anyone of any age
  • No account creation or registration is required or possible
  • No child's information is ever transmitted off their device

6. Third-Party Services

The only external services this site connects to are:

  • Google Fonts — to load the Nunito and Fredoka One typefaces. Google's privacy policy applies to these font requests.
  • OpenAI API — only when you voluntarily use an AI feature with your own API key (see Section 4).
  • GitHub Pages — our hosting provider. GitHub may log standard server access logs (IP address, browser type) as part of normal hosting operations.

We have no advertising partners, affiliate relationships, or data brokers.

7. Data Security

Since we store no data on any server we control, there is no server-side data to breach. All data lives in your browser's localStorage and is subject to your device's own security.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Visit our Parent Guide or open an issue on our GitHub repository.
